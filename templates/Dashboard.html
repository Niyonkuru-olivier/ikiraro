Determinants of Performance

The synthesis below triangulates crop-cutting data, seasonal climate outlooks, and policy execution reports to attribute yield movements:

  • Weather: Rainfall anomalies explain 38% of the variance in maize outcomes and 29% for beans. Irrigation buffers districts in Eastern Province, narrowing downside scenarios.
  • Fertilizers: Blended inputs (NPK 17:17:17) improve maize yields by 0.4 t/ha on average when adoption is above 60% of targeted households.
  • Government Policy: Subsidy disbursement timing and extension agent coverage remain the largest differentiators of district-level performance.
